\syllabussection{Course Description}
A first course in differential and integral calculus of one variable:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Limits and continuity
	\item The derivative: definition, rules, and the chain rule
	\item Applications of the derivative (related rates, optimization)
	\item The definite integral and the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus
\end{itemize}
