\syllabussection{Course Description}
The following topics will be covered in this course:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Limits and continuity
	\item Derivatives and the rules of differentiation
	\item Applications of derivatives (related rates, optimization)
	\item Integrals and the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus
	\item Transcendental functions and their calculus
\end{itemize}
