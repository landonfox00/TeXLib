\begin{itemize}
	\item Notes, calculators of any kind, and other aids are not permitted.
	\item Show all work. Guessing and checking is not an accepted method --- a clear process or reasoning must be demonstrated.
	\item Leave all answers in exact form (e.g., as simplified fractions or with radicals) unless otherwise instructed.
	\item Box or circle your final answer. Do not box or circle multiple answers.
	\item Partial credit will be awarded. If you are unsure how to approach a problem, write down any relevant information, formulas, or partial work.
	\item If you have any questions, raise your hand or approach the instructor.
\end{itemize}
