The University of Nevada, Reno is committed to providing a safe learning and work
environment for all. If you believe you have experienced discrimination, sexual
harassment, sexual assault, domestic/dating violence, or stalking, whether on or
off campus or need information related to immigration concerns, please contact
the University's \href{https://www.unr.edu/civil-rights}{Center for Civil Rights
\& Equal Access} at (775) 784-1547 or CCR@unr.edu.
