Any student who has difficulty affording groceries or accessing sufficient food,
or who lacks a safe and stable place to live, is encouraged to contact
\href{https://www.unr.edu/student-engagement/support-services/pack-provisions}{Pack
Provisions}, an ASUN program that provides food and basic-need resources to members
of the University community.
