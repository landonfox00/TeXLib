% UNR Statement on Audio and Video Recording (UAM 6,501), student-recording
% prohibition. Default; override via the coursemeta statements-path (recording.tex).
Surreptitious or covert video-taping of class or unauthorized audio recording of
class is prohibited by law and by Board of Regents policy. This class may be
videotaped or audio recorded only with the written permission of the instructor.
In order to accommodate students with disabilities, some students may have been
given permission to record class lectures and discussions. Therefore, students
should understand that their comments during class may be recorded.
