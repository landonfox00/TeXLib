\syllabussection{Course Description}
The following topics will be covered in this course:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Fundamentals of algebra
	\item Polynomial, rational, exponential, and logarithmic functions
	\item Complex numbers
	\item Polynomial and rational inequalities
	\item Systems of equations
\end{itemize}
