\syllabussection{Course Description}
The following topics will be covered in this course:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Fundamentals of algebra
	\item Polynomial, rational, exponential, and logarithmic functions, their graphs, and applications
	\item Complex numbers
	\item Polynomial and rational inequalities
	\item Systems of equations
\end{itemize}

\syllabussection{Student Learning Outcomes}
Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:
\begin{enumerate}
	\item Graph rational functions.
	\item Solve equations that involve exponential or logarithmic functions.
	\item Solve inequalities that involve rational functions.
\end{enumerate}
This course satisfies the university core mathematics requirement and meets the Core Objective 2: Quantitative Reasoning.
